Description

Gunfire Reborn is an adventure level-based game featuring FPS, roguelite, and RPG elements. Players can control unique heroes - each with different abilities - as they adventure through procedurally-generated levels and pick up randomly-dropped weapons.

You can play Gunfire Reborn alone or cooperatively with up to three other players (4-player co-op).
